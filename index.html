About Alan

Alan grew up with his father Tom, mother Ann, and older brother Bennett (along with a houseful of wildlife) in North Carolina. After graduating from North Carolina State University, he moved to Japan for a year to study the language (and meet Yuka). In 2009 he moved to Seattle to work at Nintendo.

About Yuka

Yuka grew up with her father Atsushi, mother Mitsuko, and older brother Takashi in Nagano, Japan. She then moved to Kanagawa to attend Waseda University in Tokyo. After graduating in 2010, she moved to Seattle to attend graduate school at the University of Washington.
