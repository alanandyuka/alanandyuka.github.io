Parsons Garden

The wedding ceremony will be held at Parsons Garden in Queen Anne, Seattle. The garden is very special to us since we used to walk here often when Yuka lived in Queen Anne, and it’s also where Alan proposed.

 

The garden is tucked away on the corner of a Highland Drive, a beautiful residential street in Queen Anne. It’s just a few blocks down the street from the much more famous Kerry Park where you can get the best views of Seattle.

 

The address is 7th Ave W & W Highland Drive. If you’re driving, you can park anywhere on the nearby streets. If you’re taking public transportation while visiting, the 2 and 13 buses stop close by on Queen Anne Ave and Highland Drive.
